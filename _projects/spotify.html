New Spotify Social Features

The Challenge

Sometimes designers work with companies with an established identity. So how do they help them innovate?

By bringing new perspectives, ideas and, mainly, by creating new features for the product. Thus designers help brands stand out and keep up with what people want and need. We took a deeper look at Spotify and decided to spice it up.

Why Spotify

Spotify is the most popular music and podcast streaming platform in the world. It focuses on giving a simple and intuitive experience of listening and finding music.
